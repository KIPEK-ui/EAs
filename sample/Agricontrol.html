Our Quality Standards

Tea Quality Standards

  • Leaf appearance: Uniform color, no discoloration
  • Aroma: Fresh, characteristic of tea type
  • Infusion color: Bright and clear
  • Taste: Well-balanced, no off-flavors
  • Moisture content: ≤6%

Coffee Quality Standards

  • Bean size: Uniform, minimum 90% screen size 16+
  • Defects: ≤5 full defects per 300g
  • Aroma: Distinctive, no musty or fermented notes
  • Cupping score: ≥85 points (SCAA scale)
  • Moisture content: 10-12%

Macadamia Quality Standards

  • Kernel recovery: ≥35%
  • Color: Creamy white to light ivory
  • Texture: Crisp, not rubbery
  • Oil content: ≥72%
  • Foreign matter: ≤0.5%

Avocado Quality Standards

  • Size: 6-18 oz depending on variety
  • Color: Characteristic of variety when ripe
  • Firmness: Appropriate for stage of ripeness
  • Dry matter content: ≥21% (Hass)
  • Defects: ≤5% surface area affected
